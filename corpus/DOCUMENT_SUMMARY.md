## Citation Best Practices

When the RAG chatbot answers questions, it should:

1. **Cite the source document**
   - Example: "According to our Privacy Policy..."

2. **Include section when relevant**
   - Example: "As stated in the Shipping Policy (International Shipping section)..."

3. **Provide direct quotes for policies**
   - Example: "Our Terms of Service state: '[exact quote]'"

4. **Reference tables when applicable**
   - Example: "Based on our pricing table, a hardcover book costs $39.99"

5. **Offer related information**
   - Example: "For more details, see our Story Customization Guide"

6. **Suggest contact for uncovered topics**
   - Example: "For specific questions not covered here, contact support@customkidsbooks.com"
