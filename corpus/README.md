# Corpus Documentation

## Overview

This corpus contains comprehensive policy and instructional documents for Custom Children's Books, a company that creates personalized storybooks for children. The documents support a RAG-based chatbot that helps parents understand policies and create personalized stories.

## Business Description

**Custom Children's Books** creates personalized children's storybooks that help kids navigate important life moments. Parents can:
- Choose from themed stories (potty training, first day at school, first flight, doctor visits, etc.)
- Upload their child's photo to be integrated into illustrations
- Customize the story with their child's name, age, and preferences
- Add optional elements like family members, pets, and cultural customizations
- Receive digital or physical books

## RAG Chatbot Use Cases

The chatbot should be able to answer questions about:

1. **Getting Started**
   - "How do I create a personalized book?"
   - "What story themes are available?"
   - "How much does it cost?"

2. **Photo Requirements**
   - "What kind of photo should I upload?"
   - "Can I use a photo with other people in it?"
   - "What if my photo is rejected?"

3. **Privacy and Security**
   - "Is my child's photo safe?"
   - "How long do you keep photos?"
   - "Can I delete my child's information?"

4. **Customization**
   - "Can I add my child's sibling to the story?"
   - "How do I customize the story for my child's specific fear?"
   - "What customization options are included?"

5. **Ordering and Payment**
   - "What payment methods do you accept?"
   - "When will I be charged?"
   - "Are there any discounts available?"

6. **Delivery**
   - "How long until I receive my book?"
   - "Can I track my order?"
   - "Do you ship internationally?"

7. **Returns and Refunds**
   - "Can I return a personalized book?"
   - "What if my book arrives damaged?"
   - "How do I cancel my order?"

8. **Technical Issues**
   - "Why can't I upload my photo?"
   - "I can't download my digital book"
   - "My payment was declined"

9. **Account Management**
   - "How do I reset my password?"
   - "Can I order books for multiple children?"
   - "How do I delete my account?"

10. **Legal and Policy Questions**
    - "What are your terms of service?"
    - "How do you comply with COPPA?"
    - "What are my privacy rights?"

## Citation Guidelines for RAG System

When answering questions, the chatbot should:
1. Cite the specific document source
2. Include relevant section titles when applicable
3. Provide direct quotes for policy statements
4. Offer to provide more detailed information from related documents
5. Suggest contacting support for questions not covered in documents

**Example Citation Format:**
"According to our Privacy Policy (Section: Children's Privacy Rights), we comply fully with COPPA by obtaining verifiable parental consent before collecting children's information..."
