## Security Features

### Password Management

**Change password:**
1. Account Settings â†’ Security
2. Enter current password
3. Enter new password (twice)
4. Save changes

**Password requirements:**
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least 1 uppercase letter
- At least 1 number
- At least 1 special character
- Cannot reuse last 3 passwords

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

**Optional extra security:**
- Enable in Security settings
- Receive code via SMS or email
- Required at login
- Protects against unauthorized access

**Recommended for:**
- Stored payment methods
- Multiple children's data
- High-value order history

### Login History

**Monitor account access:**
- View recent logins
- See device and location
- Identify suspicious activity
- Logout all devices option

### Security Alerts

**We notify you of:**
- New device login
- Password changes
- Email address changes
- Payment method updates
- Shipping address changes

## Account Deletion

### Requesting Deletion

**To delete your account:**

1. **Account Settings** â†’ Privacy
2. **Request Account Deletion**
3. **Confirm decision** (irreversible)
4. **Verify identity** (password or email)
5. **Account deleted** within 7 business days

### What Gets Deleted

**Permanently removed:**
- Personal information
- Child profiles and photos
- Saved payment methods
- Shipping addresses
- Email preferences
- Referral credits

**What we retain:**
- Order history (7 years for tax/legal compliance)
- Transaction records (anonymized)
- Communications (if legally required)

### Before You Delete

**Consider:**
- Download your data first
- Save digital books (won't be accessible after)
- Use remaining credits
- Cancel active orders
- Cannot be undone

## Account Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

**Solutions:**

1. **Forgot password?**
   - Click "Forgot Password"
   - Enter email address
   - Check email for reset link
   - Create new password

2. **Email not recognized?**
   - Try alternate email
   - Check if you used social login
   - Contact support

3. **Account locked?**
   - Too many failed attempts
   - Wait 30 minutes
   - Or contact support

### Email Not Received

**Check:**
- Spam/junk folder
- Promotions tab (Gmail)
- Correct email address in settings
- Email not blocked

**Request resend:**
- Account Settings â†’ Resend Verification
- Or contact support

### Payment Method Issues

**Common problems:**

**"Card declined":**
- Verify card details
- Check expiration date
- Ensure sufficient funds
- Contact your bank

**"Cannot add card":**
- Try different browser
- Clear cache and cookies
- Disable browser extensions
- Contact support
