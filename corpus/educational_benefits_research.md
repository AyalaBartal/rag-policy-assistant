### Why Personalization Matters

**Psychological principles:**

**Self-reference effect:**
- Information about self is better remembered
- Personal relevance increases attention
- Deeper processing of content
- Stronger memory formation

**Identification:**
- Child identifies with character
- Increases emotional investment
- Enhances learning
- Promotes behavior modeling

**Relevance:**
- Directly applicable to child's life
- Addresses specific needs
- Timely intervention
- Practical application

## Best Practices for Maximum Benefit

### Timing

**Optimal timing:**
- 2-4 weeks before event
- Not too early (child forgets)
- Not too late (already anxious)
- Allows processing time

### Frequency

**Reading schedule:**
- Daily leading up to event
- Multiple times if child requests
- After event for reinforcement
- As needed for comfort

### Engagement

**Interactive reading:**
- Ask questions
- Discuss feelings
- Make connections
- Encourage participation

### Follow-through

**Real-world application:**
- Reference book during event
- Use book language
- Celebrate success
- Reinforce learning
