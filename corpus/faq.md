# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is Custom Children's Books?

Custom Children's Books creates personalized storybooks that feature your child as the main character. We integrate your child's name and photo into professionally illustrated stories that help children navigate important life moments like potty training, first day of school, doctor visits, and more.

### How does personalization work?

You provide your child's name, age, and photo. Our artists integrate your child's face into our illustrations while maintaining our professional art style. Your child's name appears throughout the story in natural, age-appropriate ways.

### What age range are the books for?

Our books are designed for children ages 2-8. Content is customized based on your child's age to ensure appropriate vocabulary, concepts, and story complexity.

### Are the books educational?

Yes! Each story is designed to help children understand and cope with specific life challenges or milestones. They combine engaging storytelling with gentle guidance and positive reinforcement.

## Photos and Customization

### What photo should I upload?

**Best photos are:**
- Clear and in focus
- Well-lit (natural light is best)
- Front-facing with child looking at camera
- Showing child's full face
- Simple background (we can edit backgrounds)

**Avoid:**
- Blurry or dark photos
- Side profiles or looking away
- Heavy filters or editing
- Busy backgrounds with copyrighted characters

### What if I don't have a good photo?

Take a new photo following our guidelines! Use your smartphone in good lighting, have your child look at the camera, and keep the background simple. Our team can help with minor editing.

### Can I use photos with other people in them?

Yes, but:
- We'll crop to focus on your child
- If you want others included, add them as supporting characters
- Ensure you have permission to use anyone else's image

### How many photos can I upload?

- 1 main photo required (for your child)
- Up to 3 additional photos optional (for family members, pets, settings)

### Can I change the photo after ordering?

- Before production starts: Yes, contact us immediately
- After production begins: No, you'll need to cancel and reorder
- After shipping: No changes possible

## Returns and Refunds

### Can I return a personalized book?

Personalized books are custom-made and generally non-returnable. However:
- **Defective books:** Full refund or replacement
- **Our error:** Full refund or replacement
- **Quality issues:** We'll work with you to resolve
- **Unopened books:** May be eligible for return

### What qualifies as defective?

- Printing errors or smudges
- Binding issues or loose pages
- Incorrect personalization
- Damaged upon arrival

**Not considered defects:**
- Minor color variations (normal in printing)
- Artistic interpretation of photos
- Customer's change of mind

### How do I request a refund?

1. Log into your account

3. Select the order
4. Click "Request Refund"
5. Provide reason and details
6. Submit request

Or email support@customkidsbooks.com with your order number.

### How long do refunds take?

Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method. Bank processing may take additional time.

### Can I cancel my order?

**Before production starts:** Yes, full refund
**During production:** Maybe, contact us immediately
**After shipping:** No, return policy applies

## Technical Issues

### The website isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try these steps:
1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
2. Try a different browser
3. Check your internet connection
4. Disable browser extensions temporarily
5. Try on a different device

Still having issues? Contact support@customkidsbooks.com

### I can't upload my photo. What's wrong?

Common issues:
- **File too large:** Compress to under 10MB
- **Wrong format:** Use JPG, PNG, or HEIC
- **Browser issue:** Try different browser
- **Internet connection:** Check your connection

### I didn't receive my confirmation email.

Check:
1. Spam/junk folder
2. Promotions tab (Gmail)
3. Email address spelling in your account

Still missing? Contact us with your order details.

### I can't download my digital book.

Try:
1. Check spam folder for download link
2. Log into your account and download from there
3. Try a different browser
4. Disable pop-up blockers
5. Check your internet connection

Contact support if issues persist.
