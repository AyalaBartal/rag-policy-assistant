# Getting Started Guide - Custom Children's Books

## Welcome to Our Personalized Story Service

Create magical, personalized storybooks that help your child navigate important life moments. Our stories feature your child as the main character, making learning and growth more engaging.

## How It Works

### Step 1: Choose Your Story Theme

We offer stories for common childhood challenges:
- **Potty Training Adventures** - Help your child learn bathroom independence
- **First Day at School** - Ease anxiety about starting school
- **Flying High** - Prepare for their first airplane journey
- **Doctor Visit Heroes** - Make medical appointments less scary
- **New Sibling Stories** - Adjust to a growing family
- **Bedtime Routines** - Establish healthy sleep habits
- **Sharing and Friendship** - Learn social skills

### Step 2: Personalize Your Story

**Required Information:**
- Child's first name (used throughout the story)
- Child's age (ensures age-appropriate content)
- Child's photo (clear, front-facing image preferred)

**Optional Customization:**
- Additional photos (family members, pets, favorite toys)
- Preferred pronouns (he/him, she/her, they/them)
- Skin tone and hair color adjustments
- Special interests to weave into the story

### Step 3: Review and Order

- Preview your personalized story online
- Make any final adjustments
- Choose book format (hardcover, softcover, or digital)
- Complete your order

### Step 4: Receive Your Book

- Digital versions: Available immediately after purchase
- Physical books: Shipped within 5-7 business days
- Track your order through your account dashboard

## Photo Requirements

### Child's Main Photo
- **Format:** JPG, PNG, or HEIC
- **Size:** Minimum 1MB, maximum 10MB
- **Resolution:** At least 1200x1600 pixels
- **Quality:** Clear, well-lit, front-facing
- **Background:** Any background acceptable (we'll edit as needed)

### Additional Photos (Optional)
- Same format and size requirements
- Can include family members, pets, or objects
- Maximum 3 additional photos per book

## Story Customization Options

### Name Personalization
Your child's name appears:
- In the story title
- Throughout the narrative (15-20 times)
- In dialogue with other characters
- On the dedication page

### Visual Personalization
- Child's face integrated into illustrations
- Clothing colors can match preferences
- Background settings can reflect your home or neighborhood
- Character features adjusted to match your child

### Content Personalization
- Difficulty level adjusted for age
- Cultural elements can be incorporated
- Specific fears or concerns can be addressed
- Positive reinforcement messages customized

## Age Recommendations

- **Ages 2-3:** Potty Training, Bedtime Routines
- **Ages 3-5:** First Day at School, Doctor Visits, New Sibling
- **Ages 4-6:** First Flight, Sharing and Friendship
- **Ages 5-8:** All themes available with advanced vocabulary
