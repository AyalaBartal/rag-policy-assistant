# Photo Upload and Image Policy

## Photo Submission Guidelines

### Acceptable Photo Content

**We Accept:**
- Clear photos of children (with parental consent)
- Family members who will appear in the story
- Household pets
- Favorite toys or comfort objects
- Home or neighborhood settings (for background customization)

**We Do Not Accept:**
- Photos containing other people's children without consent
- Images with visible copyrighted characters or brands
- Photos with inappropriate content or backgrounds
- Low-quality, blurry, or heavily filtered images
- Photos with watermarks or text overlays

### Technical Requirements

**File Specifications:**
- **Accepted formats:** JPG, JPEG, PNG, HEIC
- **File size:** Minimum 1MB, maximum 10MB per image
- **Resolution:** Minimum 1200x1600 pixels (higher is better)
- **Aspect ratio:** Portrait orientation preferred (3:4 or 2:3)
- **Color mode:** RGB color space

**Photo Quality Standards:**
- Well-lit with natural or soft lighting
- In focus with clear facial features
- Minimal shadows on face
- Neutral or simple backgrounds preferred
- Child looking at camera (for main photo)
- No heavy filters or editing applied

### Privacy and Consent

**Parental Consent:**
- By uploading photos, you confirm you are the parent or legal guardian
- You grant us permission to use the image solely for creating your personalized book
- You confirm the child's image is not subject to any legal restrictions

**Photo Usage:**
- Photos are used exclusively for your ordered book
- Images are not shared with third parties
- Photos are not used for marketing without explicit consent
- We do not sell or distribute customer photos

**Photo Storage:**
- Photos are stored securely on encrypted servers
- Images are retained for 90 days after order completion
- You can request photo deletion at any time
- Deleted photos are permanently removed within 7 business days

### Photo Editing and Processing

**What We Do:**
- Remove or blur backgrounds as needed
- Adjust lighting and color balance
- Crop and resize to fit illustration style
- Integrate face into story illustrations
- Minor touch-ups for print quality

**What We Don't Do:**
- Alter facial features or expressions
- Add elements not in the original photo
- Share edited images outside your order
- Use AI to generate new poses or expressions

### Multiple Photo Submissions

**Main Character Photo:**
- One primary photo required per child
- This photo is used for the main character throughout the story
- Should show child's face clearly

**Additional Photos (Optional):**
- Up to 3 additional photos per book
- Can include family members, pets, or settings
- Used for supporting characters or backgrounds
- Same quality requirements apply

### Photo Upload Process

1. **Select your story theme**
2. **Click "Upload Photos"**
3. **Choose main character photo** (required)
4. **Add optional photos** (family, pets, settings)
5. **Review uploaded images**
6. **Confirm photo quality meets requirements**
7. **Proceed to customization**

### Troubleshooting Photo Issues

**"Photo resolution too low"**
- Use a higher quality camera or phone setting
- Avoid zooming in on photos before upload
- Take a new photo in good lighting

**"File size too large"**
- Use our built-in compression tool
- Or compress before upload using free tools
- Ensure file is under 10MB

**"Photo rejected for quality"**
- Check that image is in focus
- Ensure adequate lighting
- Remove filters or heavy editing
- Take a new photo if needed

**"Background too busy"**
- We can edit backgrounds, but simpler is better
- Consider retaking with a plain wall behind child
- Outdoor photos with natural backgrounds work well

### Photo Replacement

**Before Order Completion:**
- You can replace photos anytime during customization
- Click "Change Photo" on the customization page
- Upload new image following same guidelines

**After Order Placed:**
- Photo changes require order cancellation and reorder
- Contact customer support within 24 hours
- Refund issued if book hasn't entered production

### Rights and Ownership

**Your Rights:**
- You retain all rights to your original photos
- You can request copies of edited versions
- You can use the final book images for personal use

**Our Rights:**
- Limited license to use photos for your book creation
- Right to refuse photos that violate our policies
- Right to delete photos after retention period

**Prohibited Uses:**
- We will never use your photos for marketing without consent
- Photos are not used to train AI models
- Images are not sold or licensed to third parties

### Security Measures

**Upload Security:**
- All uploads use SSL/TLS encryption
- Photos are scanned for malware
- Secure servers with regular security audits

**Access Control:**
- Only authorized production staff access photos
- All access is logged and monitored
- Staff sign confidentiality agreements

**Data Protection:**
- Compliance with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
- GDPR compliant for international customers
- Regular security assessments and updates

### Photo Deletion Requests

To request photo deletion:
1. Log into your account
2. Go to "My Orders"
3. Select the order
4. Click "Delete Photos"
5. Confirm deletion request

Or contact support@customkidsbooks.com with:
- Order number
- Email address on account
- Confirmation of deletion request

Photos will be permanently deleted within 7 business days.
