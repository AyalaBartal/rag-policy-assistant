# Privacy Policy - Custom Children's Books

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2026

## Introduction

Custom Children's Books ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and their families. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information, including children's data, in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other applicable privacy laws.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information from Parents

**Account Information:**
- Parent/guardian name
- Email address
- Phone number (optional)
- Billing address
- Shipping address
- Payment information (processed securely through third-party payment processors)

**Order Information:**
- Order history
- Book customization preferences
- Communication preferences
- Customer support interactions

### Children's Information

We collect children's information only with verifiable parental consent:

**Required Information:**
- Child's first name
- Child's age or date of birth
- Child's photo(s)

**Optional Information:**
- Preferred pronouns
- Physical characteristics (for illustration matching)
- Interests or preferences (for story customization)

### Automatically Collected Information

**Technical Data:**
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Operating system
- Pages visited and time spent
- Referring website

**Cookies and Tracking:**
- Session cookies (essential for website function)
- Analytics cookies (with consent)
- Preference cookies (to remember your settings)

## How We Use Information

### Parent Information

We use parent information to:
- Process and fulfill orders
- Communicate about orders and shipping
- Provide customer support
- Send account-related notifications
- Process payments and prevent fraud
- Improve our services and website
- Send marketing communications (with consent)

### Children's Information

We use children's information solely to:
- Create personalized storybooks
- Integrate photos into illustrations
- Customize story content appropriately
- Fulfill your order

**We do NOT:**
- Use children's information for marketing
- Share children's data with third parties for their marketing
- Create profiles of children beyond what's necessary for book creation
- Use children's photos for any purpose other than your order

### Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For EU/UK customers, we process data based on:
- **Contract performance:** To fulfill your book order
- **Consent:** For marketing communications and optional features
- **Legitimate interests:** For fraud prevention and service improvement
- **Legal obligation:** To comply with tax and legal requirements

## Parental Consent and Control

### Verifiable Parental Consent

Before collecting any child's information, we:
- Require account creation by a parent/guardian
- Verify the email address through confirmation link
- Require explicit consent checkbox for children's data collection
- Provide clear notice of what information is collected and how it's used

### Parental Rights

Parents have the right to:
- **Review** all information collected about their child
- **Request deletion** of their child's information
- **Refuse further collection** of their child's information
- **Revoke consent** at any time

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@customkidsbooks.com

### How to Request Child Data Deletion

1. Log into your account
2. Navigate to "Privacy Settings"
3. Select "Delete Child Information"
4. Confirm your request

Or email privacy@customkidsbooks.com with:
- Your account email
- Child's name
- Order number (if applicable)
- Deletion request confirmation

We will delete the information within 7 business days.

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location

- Data stored on secure servers in the United States
- Cloud storage with AWS (Amazon Web Services)
- Encrypted backups maintained for disaster recovery

### Security Measures

**Technical Safeguards:**
- SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
- Encrypted storage for photos and personal information
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Firewall protection and intrusion detection
- Secure access controls and authentication

**Organizational Safeguards:**
- Employee background checks
- Confidentiality agreements for all staff
- Limited access to personal information (need-to-know basis)
- Regular privacy and security training
- Incident response procedures

### Data Retention

**Children's Photos:**
- Retained for 90 days after order completion
- Automatically deleted after retention period
- Can be deleted earlier upon request

**Order Information:**
- Retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance
- Child's name and age retained with order records
- Photos deleted per schedule above

**Account Information:**
- Retained while account is active
- Deleted within 30 days of account closure request
- Some information retained for legal compliance

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### Service Providers

We share information with trusted service providers who help us operate:

**Payment Processors:**
- Stripe (payment processing)
- PayPal (payment processing)
- PCI-DSS compliant, do not store full card numbers

**Shipping Partners:**
- UPS, FedEx, USPS (shipping fulfillment)
- Receive only shipping address and order details
- Do not receive photos or child's personal information

**Printing Partners:**
- Receive only information necessary to print your book
- Sign confidentiality agreements
- Required to delete data after order completion

**Analytics Providers:**
- Google Analytics (anonymized data only)
- No personally identifiable information shared
- Children's information never shared

### Legal Disclosures

We may disclose information when required by law:
- In response to valid legal process (subpoena, court order)
- To protect our rights or property
- To protect safety of users or public
- In connection with business transfer or acquisition

We will notify you of legal requests unless prohibited by law.

### No Sale of Personal Information

We do NOT:
- Sell personal information to third parties
- Sell or rent email addresses
- Share children's information for third-party marketing
- Use children's data for behavioral advertising

## International Data Transfers

For customers outside the United States:
- Data may be transferred to and processed in the US
- We use Standard Contractual Clauses for EU transfers
- Adequate safeguards in place per GDPR requirements

## Children's Privacy Rights (COPPA Compliance)

### What COPPA Requires

We comply fully with COPPA by:
- Obtaining verifiable parental consent before collecting children's information
- Providing clear notice of our information practices
- Limiting collection to what's necessary for book creation
- Protecting confidentiality and security of children's information
- Allowing parents to review and delete children's information

### Age Restrictions

- Our service is intended for children under 13 with parental supervision
- Parents must create accounts and provide consent
- Children cannot create accounts independently

## Your Privacy Rights

### All Users

- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal information
- **Correction:** Update or correct inaccurate information
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your information
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a portable format
- **Opt-out:** Unsubscribe from marketing communications

### California Residents (CCPA)

Additional rights under California law:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to opt-out of sale (we don't sell information)
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

### EU/UK Residents (GDPR)

Additional rights under GDPR:
- Right to object to processing
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to lodge a complaint with supervisory authority
- Right to withdraw consent
