# Return and Refund Policy

## Return Policy Overview

Custom Children's Books are personalized products made specifically for your child. Our return policy balances customer satisfaction with the custom nature of our products.

---

## Eligibility for Returns

### Returnable Items

✅ **We Accept Returns For:**
- Defective products (printing errors, binding issues)
- Damaged items received
- Incorrect personalization (our error)
- Wrong item shipped
- Unopened books (case-by-case basis)

❌ **We Do Not Accept Returns For:**
- Customer change of mind (after opening)
- Artistic interpretation differences
- Minor color variations (normal in printing)
- Customer-provided incorrect information
- Books damaged after delivery

---

## Defective Products

### What Qualifies as Defective

**Printing Defects:**
- Smudged or blurred text
- Missing pages
- Pages printed upside down
- Significant color bleeding
- Incorrect page order

**Binding Defects:**
- Pages falling out
- Spine separating
- Cover detaching
- Loose binding

**Personalization Errors (Our Fault):**
- Wrong name printed
- Incorrect age content
- Wrong photo used
- Missing customizations paid for

### Defect Return Process

1. **Report within 30 days** of delivery
2. **Contact us** at returns@customkidsbooks.com
3. **Provide:**
   - Order number
   - Photos of defect
   - Description of issue
4. **We'll review** within 24-48 hours
5. **Resolution:**
   - Free replacement, OR
   - Full refund (including shipping)

**Timeline:**
- Replacement shipped within 3-5 business days
- Refund processed within 5-7 business days

---

## Damaged Items

### Damage During Shipping

**If package arrives damaged:**

1. **Document immediately:**
   - Photo of damaged box exterior
   - Photo of damaged book
   - Keep all packaging materials

2. **Report within 7 days:**
   - Email: returns@customkidsbooks.com
   - Include order number and photos

3. **We'll arrange:**
   - Free replacement (expedited shipping)
   - No need to return damaged item
   - Full refund if preferred

**Our Responsibility:**
- We file insurance claim with carrier
- No cost to you
- Fast resolution

---

## Wrong Item Received

### If You Received Wrong Book

**Immediate steps:**
1. Don't open the book if possible
2. Contact us immediately
3. Provide photos of what you received
4. Include your order number

**We'll provide:**
- Prepaid return label
- Correct book shipped immediately
- Expedited shipping at no charge
- Apology and possible discount on future order

**Timeline:**
- Return label sent within 24 hours
- Correct book ships upon return receipt
- Or ships immediately (your choice)

## Customer Remorse

### Unopened Books

**If you haven't opened the book:**
- Contact us within 14 days of delivery
- Must be in original condition
- Subject to 20% restocking fee
- Customer pays return shipping
- Refund issued upon receipt

**Process:**
1. Request return authorization
2. Receive return instructions
3. Ship back (your cost)
4. Refund processed within 7 days of receipt

### Opened Books

**If you've opened the book:**
- Generally not returnable
- Contact us to discuss situation
- We may offer:
  - Partial credit toward new book
  - Revision if issue is fixable
  - Goodwill discount on future order

**We want you satisfied!** Even if policy says no return, contact us. We'll work with you.

## Cancellation Policy

### Before Production Starts

**Timeline:** Usually within 24-48 hours of order

**Process:**
1. Contact us immediately
2. If production hasn't started: Full refund
3. Processed within 3-5 business days

**How to cancel:**
- Email: support@customkidsbooks.com
- Phone: 1-800-KIDBOOK
- Account dashboard: "Cancel Order" button

### During Production

**Timeline:** Days 2-7 after order

**Options:**
- May not be possible to cancel
- Partial refund available (typically 50%)
- Depends on production stage

**Contact us immediately:**
- We'll check production status
- Provide best available option
- Process as quickly as possible

### After Shipping

**Cannot cancel shipped orders**
- Return policy applies instead
- Track package and refuse delivery if possible
- Contact us for assistance

## Digital Book Refunds

### Digital Book Policy

**No refunds after download:**
- Once download link is accessed
- Digital content cannot be "returned"
- All sales final after download

**Refunds available if:**
- Technical issues prevent access
- Wrong book delivered digitally
- Significant errors in content

**Report issues within 7 days:**
- Email: digital@customkidsbooks.com
- Describe technical problem
- We'll troubleshoot or refund

## Refund Processing

### Refund Timeline

| Refund Type | Processing Time | Return to Account |
|-------------|----------------|-------------------|
| Credit card | 5-7 business days | 3-5 business days |
| PayPal | 3-5 business days | 1-2 business days |
| Gift card | 1-2 business days | Immediate |

**Total time:** 4-12 business days depending on payment method and bank

### Refund Method

**Refunds issued to original payment method:**
- Credit card → Same credit card
- PayPal → PayPal account
- Gift card → New gift card code

**Cannot refund to different method** (fraud prevention)

### Partial Refunds

**When applicable:**
- Restocking fees
- Partial cancellations
- Promotional discounts deducted
- Shipping costs excluded

**Calculation example:**
```
Original order:           $50.00
Shipping:                 $4.99
Promo discount:          -$5.00
Total paid:              $49.99

Refund (80%):            $40.00
Shipping:                $0.00
Promo (not refunded):    $0.00
Total refund:            $40.00
```
