## Delivery Problems and Solutions

### Package Not Received

**If tracking shows delivered but you didn't receive:**

1. **Check all locations** (24 hours):
   - All entrances to your home
   - Mailbox or parcel locker
   - With neighbors
   - Building office/reception
   - Garage or side doors

2. **Verify address** (24 hours):
   - Confirm correct address in order
   - Check with household members
   - Contact building management

3. **Contact carrier** (48 hours):
   - Request delivery confirmation details
   - GPS location of delivery
   - Photo proof of delivery (if available)

4. **Contact us** (after 48 hours):
   - We'll file claim with carrier
   - Investigate delivery
   - Arrange replacement or refund

### Damaged Package

**If package arrives damaged:**

1. **Document damage immediately:**
   - Take photos of box exterior
   - Take photos of book damage
   - Keep all packaging materials

2. **Contact us within 7 days:**
   - Email photos to support@customkidsbooks.com
   - Include order number
   - Describe damage

3. **We'll arrange:**
   - Replacement book (no charge)
   - Return shipping label (if needed)
   - Expedited replacement shipping

### Lost Package

**If tracking shows no movement for 7+ days:**

1. **Contact carrier first:**
   - They have most current information
   - Can initiate trace request

2. **Contact us after carrier investigation:**
   - We'll file insurance claim
   - Send replacement immediately
   - No additional charge to you

### Wrong Item Received

**If you received wrong book or someone else's order:**

1. **Contact us immediately:**
   - Don't open the book if possible
   - Provide photos of what you received
   - Include your order number

2. **We'll arrange:**
   - Return shipping label (prepaid)
   - Correct book sent immediately
   - Expedited shipping at no charge
