# Story Customization Guide

## Overview

This guide explains all the ways you can personalize your child's storybook to create a truly unique and meaningful experience.

## Basic Customization (Included in All Books)

### Name Integration

**How It Works:**
- Your child's name appears throughout the story
- Used in dialogue, narration, and character interactions
- Appears 15-20 times in age-appropriate contexts
- Featured prominently on the cover and title page

**Name Guidelines:**
- First name only (middle names optional)
- Maximum 15 characters
- Special characters and accents supported
- Nicknames acceptable

**Examples:**
- "Emma was excited for her first day of school."
- "The teacher smiled and said, 'Welcome, Emma!'"
- "Emma's First Day at School" (title)

### Age Appropriateness

**Content Adjusted By Age:**
- **Ages 2-3:** Simple sentences, basic concepts, 12-16 pages
- **Ages 3-5:** Short paragraphs, expanded vocabulary, 16-20 pages
- **Ages 5-8:** Complex sentences, detailed storylines, 20-28 pages

**Reading Level:**
- Vocabulary matched to developmental stage
- Sentence complexity appropriate for age
- Concepts and themes age-relevant

### Photo Integration

**Main Character:**
- Your child's face integrated into illustrations
- Appears on every page or every other page (depending on story)
- Consistent character representation throughout
- Professional illustration style maintained

**Quality Assurance:**
- Our artists review each integration
- Adjustments made for best visual result
- Preview provided before final production

## Advanced Customization Options

### Physical Appearance

**Included Adjustments:**
- Skin tone matching
- Hair color and style matching
- Eye color matching
- General facial features

**Optional Enhancements ($5 each):**
- Specific clothing colors or styles
- Glasses or accessories
- Hairstyle modifications
- Freckles or other distinctive features

**How to Specify:**
- Use the customization form during order
- Upload reference photos if needed
- Provide detailed descriptions
- Our artists will match as closely as possible

### Pronoun Selection

**Available Options:**
- He/Him
- She/Her
- They/Them

**Implementation:**
- Pronouns used consistently throughout story
- Grammar adjusted appropriately
- No additional cost

### Supporting Characters

**Family Members ($10 per character):**
- Add parents, siblings, or grandparents
- Upload photos for each character
- Specify relationship and name
- Integrated into story naturally

**Pets ($8 per pet):**
- Add family dog, cat, or other pet
- Upload pet photo
- Specify pet name and type
- Appears in 2-3 scenes

**Friends ($10 per friend):**
- Add a best friend character
- Upload friend's photo (with parent permission)
- Specify friend's name
- Plays supporting role in story

### Setting Customization

**Home Environment ($15):**
- Customize background settings to match your home
- Upload photos of your house, yard, or neighborhood
- Artists incorporate recognizable elements
- Creates familiar, comforting environment

**Cultural Elements ($12):**
- Incorporate cultural traditions or celebrations
- Add cultural clothing or decorations
- Include specific cultural references
- Respectful and authentic representation

### Story Modifications

**Difficulty Level Adjustment (Free):**
- Simplify for younger readers
- Add complexity for advanced readers
- Adjust within age range

**Specific Concerns ($20):**
- Address particular fears or anxieties
- Emphasize specific learning goals
- Customize messaging for your child's needs
- Consultation with our content team

**Examples:**
- Fear of loud noises (for doctor visit story)
- Separation anxiety (for school story)
- Specific phobia (for flight story)

### Dedication Page

**Standard Dedication (Free):**
- "Made especially for [Child's Name]"
- Date of creation

**Custom Dedication ($8):**
- Personal message from you (up to 100 words)
- Special occasion note (birthday, achievement, etc.)
- Message from specific family members
- Inspirational quote or blessing

**Example:**
"To our brave little Emma,
You are stronger than you know and braver than you believe. We're so proud of you as you start this new adventure. Remember, we're always here for you.
Love, Mom and Dad"

**Customizable Elements:**

- Specific challenges your child faces
- Favorite bathroom books or toys

**Story Variations:**
- Daytime training focus

### First Day at School

**Customizable Elements:**
- Type of school (preschool, kindergarten, elementary)

- Teacher's name (generic or specific)
- Classroom activities your child will experience

**Story Variations:**
- First day ever vs. new school
- Full day vs. half day program

- Focus on separation from parents

**Customizable Elements:**
- Destination (beach, grandparents, Disney, etc.)

- Specific flight concerns (takeoff, landing, turbulence)
- Airport and airplane details

**Story Variations:**

- Domestic vs. international
- First time flying vs. building confidence

### Doctor Visit Heroes

## Customization Tips

### For Best Results

**Photos:**
- Use high-quality, well-lit photos
- Front-facing photos work best
- Simple backgrounds preferred
- Multiple angles helpful for artists

**Descriptions:**
- Be specific about what matters most
- Mention unique features or characteristics
- Explain your child's personality if relevant
- Share specific concerns or goals

**Timing:**
- Order 2-3 weeks before needed date
- Allow time for preview and revisions
- Rush orders available for additional fee

## Customization Limitations

### What We Can Do

- Integrate photos into our illustration style
- Adjust story content within theme framework
- Add supporting characters
- Modify settings and backgrounds
- Adjust reading level within age range

### What We Cannot Do

- Create entirely new story themes (choose from available themes)
- Use copyrighted characters (Disney, Marvel, etc.)
- Guarantee exact photo replication (artistic interpretation)
- Make unlimited revisions (one round included, additional $15 each)
- Rush production under 3 days
