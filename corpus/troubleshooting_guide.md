# Troubleshooting Guide

## Common Issues and Solutions

This guide helps you resolve common issues when creating your personalized children's book.

## Photo Upload Issues

### Problem: "Photo file size too large"

**Solution:**
1. **Use our compression tool:**
   - Click "Compress Photo" on upload page
   - Automatically reduces file size while maintaining quality
   
2. **Compress before uploading:**
   - Mac: Open in Preview > Export > Reduce Quality slider
   - Windows: Open in Photos > Resize > Save
   - Online tools: TinyPNG.com, CompressJPEG.com

3. **Take a new photo:**
   - Reduce camera resolution in phone settings
   - Use "Medium" or "High" quality instead of "Maximum"

**Maximum file size:** 10MB per image

---

### Problem: "Photo resolution too low"

**Error message:** "Image resolution must be at least 1200x1600 pixels"

**Solution:**
1. **Check photo resolution:**
   - Right-click photo > Properties > Details (Windows)
   - Open in Preview > Tools > Adjust Size (Mac)

2. **Use a better quality photo:**
   - Take new photo with higher resolution camera
   - Use phone's highest quality setting
   - Avoid screenshots or downloaded images

3. **Don't zoom or crop excessively:**
   - Use original photo without heavy editing
   - Minimal cropping preserves resolution

**Minimum resolution:** 1200x1600 pixels

---

### Problem: "Photo rejected for quality"

**Reasons for rejection:**
- Blurry or out of focus
- Too dark or poorly lit
- Face obscured or not visible
- Heavy filters applied
- Copyrighted characters visible

**Solution:**
1. **Take a new photo with:**
   - Good natural lighting (near window, outdoors)
   - Camera focused on child's face
   - Child looking at camera
   - Simple, clean background
   - No filters or heavy editing

2. **Photo checklist:**
   - ✓ Clear and in focus
   - ✓ Well-lit
   - ✓ Front-facing
   - ✓ Full face visible
   - ✓ Simple background
   - ✗ No filters
   - ✗ No copyrighted characters

---

### Problem: "Upload keeps failing"

**Solution:**
1. **Check internet connection:**
   - Ensure stable connection
   - Try different network if available
   - Avoid uploading on slow/unstable connections

2. **Try different browser:**
   - Chrome (recommended)
   - Firefox
   - Safari
   - Edge

3. **Clear browser cache:**
   - Chrome: Settings > Privacy > Clear browsing data
   - Firefox: Options > Privacy > Clear Data
   - Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data

4. **Disable browser extensions:**
   - Ad blockers may interfere
   - Privacy extensions may block uploads
   - Try incognito/private mode

5. **Try different device:**
   - Upload from computer instead of phone
   - Or vice versa

---

### Problem: "Wrong photo uploaded"

**Before order completion:**
1. Click "Change Photo" on customization page
2. Upload correct photo
3. Confirm replacement

**After order placed but before production:**
1. Contact support immediately: support@customkidsbooks.com
2. Provide order number
3. Upload correct photo
4. We'll update if production hasn't started

**After production started:**
- Cannot change photo
- Must cancel order and reorder
- Contact support for assistance

---

## Account and Login Issues

### Problem: "Can't log in / Forgot password"

**Solution:**
1. **Reset password:**
   - Click "Forgot Password" on login page
   - Enter your email address
   - Check email for reset link (check spam folder)
   - Create new password

2. **Check email address:**
   - Ensure correct email address
   - Try alternate email if you have multiple

3. **Account may not exist:**
   - Click "Sign Up" to create new account
   - Check if you used social login (Google, Facebook)

---

### Problem: "Didn't receive verification email"

**Solution:**
1. **Check spam/junk folder**
2. **Check promotions tab** (Gmail users)
3. **Wait 10 minutes** (emails may be delayed)
4. **Request new verification email:**
   - Log in attempt will prompt resend option
   - Or contact support

5. **Check email address spelling:**
   - Log in and check account settings
   - Update if incorrect

---

### Problem: "Can't create account"

**Common issues:**

**"Email already in use":**
- Account already exists with this email
- Try logging in or reset password
- Check if you used social login

**"Password doesn't meet requirements":**
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character

**"Invalid email address":**
- Check for typos
- Ensure proper email format (name@domain.com)

## Order and Payment Issues

### Problem: "Payment declined"

**Solution:**
1. **Check card details:**
   - Card number correct
   - Expiration date valid
   - CVV code correct
   - Billing address matches card

2. **Contact your bank:**
   - May be flagged as suspicious
   - Confirm transaction with bank
   - Try again after approval

3. **Try different payment method:**
   - Different credit card
   - PayPal
   - Apple Pay or Google Pay

4. **Check card limits:**
   - Ensure sufficient funds
   - Check daily spending limits
   - Verify international transaction allowed (if applicable)

---

### Problem: "Promo code not working"

**Common reasons:**

**"Invalid code":**
- Check spelling and spacing
- Codes are case-sensitive
- Ensure no extra spaces

**"Code expired":**
- Check expiration date
- Request new code if needed

**"Code already used":**
- Most codes single-use per account
- Cannot reuse same code

**"Minimum purchase not met":**
- Some codes require minimum order amount
- Check code terms and conditions

**"Cannot combine with other offers":**
- Only one promo code per order
- Cannot stack multiple discounts

---

### Problem: "Order confirmation not received"

**Solution:**
1. **Check spam/junk folder**
2. **Check promotions tab** (Gmail)
3. **Log into account:**
   - Go to "My Orders"
   - Verify order appears there
   - If yes, order was successful

4. **Check email address:**
   - Verify correct email in account settings
   - Update if needed

5. **Contact support:**
   - Provide order details
   - We'll resend confirmation

---

### Problem: "Can't find my order"

**Solution:**
1. **Log into correct account:**
   - Verify email address used
   - Check if you have multiple accounts

2. **Check order history:**
   - Account dashboard > "My Orders"
   - Use search or filter options

3. **Check email for order number:**
   - Search inbox for "Custom Children's Books"
   - Order number in confirmation email

4. **Contact support:**
   - Provide email address and approximate order date
   - We'll locate your order

## Shipping and Delivery Issues

### Problem: "Tracking number not working"

**Solution:**
1. **Wait 24 hours:**
   - Tracking may not activate immediately
   - Carrier needs to scan package

2. **Check correct carrier:**
   - Verify which shipping service (UPS, FedEx, USPS)
   - Use correct tracking website

3. **Copy/paste tracking number:**
   - Avoid typos
   - Remove extra spaces

4. **Contact support:**
   - We'll verify tracking information
   - Provide update on shipment status

---

### Problem: "Package delayed"

**Solution:**
1. **Check tracking for updates:**
   - Carrier may provide delay reason
   - Weather, holidays, or carrier issues

2. **Wait for carrier delivery:**
   - Most delays resolve within 2-3 days

3. **Contact carrier:**
   - They have most current information
   - Can provide specific delivery updates

4. **Contact us if significantly delayed:**
   - After 7 days past expected delivery
   - We'll investigate and assist

---

### Problem: "Package marked delivered but not received"

**Solution:**
1. **Check all possible locations:**
   - Front door, back door, side entrance
   - Mailbox or package locker
   - With neighbors
   - Building office or reception

2. **Check with household members:**
   - Someone else may have received it

3. **Wait 24 hours:**
   - Sometimes marked delivered early
   - May arrive next day

4. **Contact carrier:**
   - Request delivery confirmation details
   - GPS location of delivery

5. **Contact us after 48 hours:**
   - We'll file claim with carrier
   - Arrange replacement or refund

---

### Problem: "Wrong shipping address"

**Before shipping:**
1. Contact support immediately
2. Provide correct address
3. We'll update if not yet shipped

**After shipping:**
- Cannot change address
- Contact carrier to attempt redirect (may incur fee)
- May need to reorder to correct address

**Prevention:**
- Always verify address before completing order
- Update address in account settings

## Digital Book Issues

### Problem: "Can't download digital book"

**Solution:**
1. **Check email for download link:**
   - Look in spam/junk folder
   - Search for "Custom Children's Books"

2. **Download from account:**
   - Log in > "My Books" > "Download"

3. **Check internet connection:**
   - Ensure stable connection
   - Large file may take time

4. **Try different browser:**
   - Some browsers handle downloads differently

5. **Disable pop-up blockers:**
   - May prevent download window

6. **Try different device:**
   - Computer vs. mobile

7. **Contact support:**
   - We'll send direct download link

---

### Problem: "Can't open PDF file"

**Solution:**
1. **Install PDF reader:**
   - Adobe Acrobat Reader (free)
   - Preview (Mac, built-in)
   - Edge or Chrome (built-in PDF viewers)

2. **Update PDF reader:**
   - Ensure latest version installed

3. **Try different PDF reader:**
   - If one doesn't work, try another

4. **Check file download:**
   - File may be corrupted
   - Re-download from account

5. **Check device compatibility:**
   - Ensure device supports PDF files

---

### Problem: "PDF looks wrong on device"

**Solution:**
1. **Zoom/scaling issues:**
   - Adjust zoom level in PDF reader
   - Try "Fit to page" or "Fit to width"

2. **Color looks different:**
   - Screen color calibration varies
   - Print version will match our standards

3. **Text appears blurry:**
   - Zoom in for better clarity
   - PDF is high resolution for printing

4. **Pages out of order:**
   - Re-download file
   - May be PDF reader issue
