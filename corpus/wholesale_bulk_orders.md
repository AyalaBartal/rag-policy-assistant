## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What's the minimum for bulk pricing?**
A: 3 books qualify for 15% discount. Significant savings start at 25+ books.

**Q: Can we mix themes in one order?**
A: Yes! Bulk pricing applies to total quantity, regardless of themes.

**Q: How long does bulk production take?**
A: 2-6 weeks depending on quantity and customization level.

**Q: Do you offer samples?**
A: Yes, we can provide a sample book for orders of 50+ books.

**Q: Can we pay with a purchase order?**
A: Yes, for approved educational institutions and established accounts.

**Q: Is there a setup fee?**
A: Only for organizational branding ($50). No other setup fees.

**Q: Can books ship to multiple addresses?**
A: Yes, additional coordination fee applies.

**Q: Do you offer fundraising programs?**
A: Yes! Schools earn 20% of sales through our fundraising program.
